**IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENTS**

Based on the latest CDC and University System of Georgia guidance, KSU’s testing center will soon increase our in-person testing capacity.  Beginning June 1, 2021, we ask all of you, our valued customers, to comply with the following guidance:

  • Based on guidance from the University System of Georgia (USG), all vaccinated and unvaccinated indviduals are encoureaged to wear a face covering while inside campus facilities.  Unvaccinated individuals are also encouraged to socially distance while inside campus facilites, when possible.
     

As an added precaution, we will continue to maintain high cleanliness and sanitation standards. We are excited about the opportunity to serve more customers in person at our world-class testing center.

Because of these changes, all exams will be available in-person at our testing center, beginning June 1, 2021.  

We are also limiting “walk-in” appointments, so please contact the testing team at ksutesting@kennesaw.edu if you have an emergency testing requirement.

For more information, the best way to communicate with the testing staff is by email at ksutesting@kennesaw.edu.

Check back for more updates.

There is now a one-time $5 annual registration fee charge applied to the cost of your first exam. For each additional exam, you will not be charged a registration fee. To ensure you are only charged once for the annual registration fee, please use the same email address for all your future exams.

Please note: If the exam cost is $0, there will be a one-time $5 annual registration fee charge applied to your first exam.

 

Kennesaw State University Academic Testing Services is a team of professionals dedicated to providing world-class testing support to the students and faculty of the Owl Nation, to non-KSU students, and to working professionals in the Greater Atlanta Metro Area. We provide a wide menu of tests to meet our customers' demands while offering fully automated, user-friendly systems to reduce pre-test anxiety. The KSU Testing Center is a certified NCTA (National College Testing Association) testing center and the testing staff adheres to all NCTA Professional Standards and Guidelines.
