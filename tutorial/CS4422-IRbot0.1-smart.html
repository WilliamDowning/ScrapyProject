SMART Center

For Students. By Students.

Free tutoring across more than 80 subjects available for all KSU students.

All SMART Center locations are OPEN!

The SMART Center at KSU provides tutoring for all current KSU students in Math, Science, Engineering, Humanities, and Social Science courses.

Services

  • In-Person Drop-In

    Stop in at one of our on campus locations to get detailed help or just to work on homework.

  • In-Appointments

    Beginning Soon. Schedule 50 minute one-on-one appointments in any of our supported courses. Please check back with us for updated information and instructions. 

  • Online Drop-In

    Visit our Virtual SMART Center for late night questions from wherever you are.

  • Workshops

    Returning Soon. Workshops on everything from the quadratic formula to test anxiety.
