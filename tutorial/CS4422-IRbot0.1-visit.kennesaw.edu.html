In-Person Campus Tours

KSU has safely offered campus tours throughout the COVID-19 pandemic because we know how much the in-person campus experience means to you.

  • In guidance with CDC guidelines, fully vaccinated individuals may resume on-campus activities without wearing a face mask, including indoors. Unvaccinated individuals are strongly encouraged to continue to socially distance from others when possible and wear face mask while inside campus facilities.

  • There is a firm guest policy in place to ensure social distancing guidelines. Students attending a campus tour are only permitted to bring 3 additional guests.

If you’re not comfortable coming on campus, or you do not have availability to take a campus tour, we encourage you to experience our virtual tour and sign up for a 1-on-1 appointment with your admissions counselor.

 

Come experience the energy, culture and passion of being an owl!

Visiting campus is a great opportunity to explore KSU and learn about Owl Nation.

Kennesaw State is a student-centered, research-driven university with a vibrant campus culture and an entrepreneurial spirit. As Owls, we are guided by a purpose and, no matter how we change, grow, or evolve, we never forget who we are. Welcome to Owl Nation!

    Campus Tours

    Designed specifically for high school students and their families. These tours highlight University Housing, on-campus dining, academic programs and undergraduate admissions.

    Spotlight Tours

    These tours are an extension of a regular campus tour where you will meet with the featured program.

    High School Group Tours

    High schools looking to visit campus as a group can request a walking tour. Group tours must be preapproved and are scheduled based on availability.

    Tour the Campus of My Major

    Between our two campuses, we offer more than 150 programs. Tours are offered on both campuses based on the major location.
