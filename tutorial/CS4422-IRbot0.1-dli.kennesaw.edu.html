  • Instructional Technology
    Explore the instructional tools available to all KSU faculty members. 
  • Open Educational Resources
    Open Educational Resources (OER) are any type of educational materials that are in the public domain or introduced with an open license. Anyone can legally and freely copy, use, adapt and re-share them, depending on the license. OER range from textbooks to curricula, syllabi, lecture notes, assignments, tests, projects, audio, video, and animation.
