    Corporate Learning: Leverage the Power of Partnership

    REQUEST A CONSULTATION

    Kennesaw State University is dedicated to helping you and your organization succeed. Using our subject-matter experts, along with our Corporate Learning team, we will design new programs that meet your business goals and objectives while leveraging our award-winning programs to help transform your business.

    Our programs use best practices in customization, technology-enhanced learning, and on-the-job application. Our classroom-based certificate programs, virtual-based programs, and digital badge mobile-learning programs are designed to help you reach your full potential as leaders, innovators, and change-makers.

    Custom-Designed Programs

    We specialize in designing hybrid programs that integrate the best practices in “GAME-fied” online learning with the best practices in classroom application. It’s called the PLAY—PRACTICE—PURPOSE approach to professional development. This innovative approach allows us to both improve and accelerate the teaching and learning process.

    Corporate Academy: Interested in Developing Your Own Learning Academy?

    A Corporate Academy serves a strategic function by integrating employee development as individuals, teams, and eventually as the developer of the entire organization.

    Corporate academies drive the educational goals of organizations that want to enhance their learning culture.  The idea is to have a classroom or virtual platform that serves as the organization’s specific and recognized training base to align employees with the organization’s business objectives.

    THE FUNDAMENTALS OF
    PROFESSIONAL SELLING

    DESCRIPTION: The professional selling certification process assists companies in:

    • Driving new revenue
    • Reducing salesforce turnover
    • Improving ramp-up time for new hires

    The key topics include:

    • Developing the mindset of top performing salespeople
    • Building the foundational selling process and the required soft skills
    • Understanding the buyer’s mindset
    • Prospecting strategies and skills
    • Presenting value-driven solutions
    • Managing customer objections
    • Successfully gaining commitment

    This program provides a foundation for individuals just beginning their sales career as well as new approaches for veteran salespeople.
