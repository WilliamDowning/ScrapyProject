    Workshop Series

     

    See our Workshops, Clubs, Events Calendar for dates and times.

    • Writing at the University Level

      Writing essays at the university level can feel overwhelming. Come to this workshop to gain a better understanding of the expectations of university-level writing. You'll learn how to organize your ideas effectively, and you'll leave with tips for effective revision and proofreading.

      • Dates

        Tuesday, 8/23, at 3 PM in EB 242 (Kennesaw)

        Thursday, 8/25, at 11AM online via Teams

        Tuesday, 9/6, at 11AM online via Teams

        Wednesday, 9/7, at 11AM online via Teams

      • Annotated Bibliographies & Literature Reviews

        Managing the research needed to tackle these unique assignments can be challenging. This workshop will teach writing and organizational strategies that will help you make sense of your sources. 

        • Dates

          Monday, 9/26, at 3 PM in EB 242 (Kennesaw)

          Thursday, 9/27, at 11AM online via Teams

        • Integrating Sources and Avoiding Plagiarism

          Come to this workshop to learn how to incorporate researched information into your writing. By learning to integrate sources effectively, you will boost your own credibility as a writer.

          • Dates

            Wednesday, 11/2, at 3PM in EB 242 (Kennesaw)

            Thursday, 11/3, at 3PM in C 101 (Marietta)

            Friday, 11/4, at 11AM online via Teams

          • Organizing and Analyzing Your Research

            Come to this workshop to learn how to stay organized and engage with your sources throughout the processes of conducting research and writing your paper. 

            • Dates

              Thursday, 9/8, at 3PM in EB 242 (Kennesaw)

              Tuesday, 9/13, at 3 PM online via Teams

              Wednesday, 9/14, at 11 AM online via Teams

            • Narrative Essay

              Are you working on a narrative essay? Come to this workshop to learn tips and tricks for writing this unique type of creative nonfiction. 

              • Dates

                Wednesday, 8/31, at 3PM in EB 242 (Kennesaw)

                Thursday, 9/1, at 3 PM online via Teams

                Friday, 9/2, at 11 PM online via Teams

              • Rhetorical Analysis

                Are you writing a rhetorical analysis essay? Come to this workshop to learn how to evaluate a text rhetorically and write about how the author's use of rhetorical strategies impacts the success or failure of the argument.

                • Dates

                  Wednesday, 10/12, at 3PM in EB 242 (Kennesaw)

                  Thursday, 10/13, at 3 PM in C 101 (Marietta)

                  Friday, 10/14, at 12 PM online via Teams

                • Argument Essays

                  Writing an argument can be challenging. Come to this workshop for an overview of common types of argument, as well as tips and tricks for writing an effective argument essay.  

                  • Dates

                    Monday, 10/24, at 3PM in EB 242 (Kennesaw)

                    Tuesday, 10/25, at 3 PM in C 101 (Marietta)

                    Tuesday, 11/1, at 11AM online via Teams

                   

                  • Scholarship Application Essays

                    Come to this workshop to learn how to write a scholarship application essay that will distinguish you from other applicants so you can earn money to help pay for your education.

                    • Dates

                      Monday, 10/17, at 3PM in EB 242 (Kennesaw)

                      Tuesday, 10/18, at 3PM in C 101 (Marietta)

                      Wednesday, 10/19, at 11AM online via Teams

                    • Using Writing to Manage Stress

                      Feeling anxious? Learn a research-backed method of writing that will help you manage stress, organize your thoughts, and find meaning in your experiences. This workshop is brought to you by KSU Counseling and Psychological Services and the KSU Writing Center.

                      • Hours

                        Thursday, 9/29, at 11AM online via Teams

                        Friday, 9/30, at 11AM online via Teams

                        Monday, 11/14, at 3PM in EB 242 (Kennesaw)

                        Tuesday, 11/15 at 3PM in C 101 (Marietta)

                        Friday, 12/2, at 11AM online via Teams

                        • Dates

                          Wednesday, 9/21, at 3 PM in EB 242 (Kennesaw)

                          Thursday, 9/22, at 5 PM online via Teams
