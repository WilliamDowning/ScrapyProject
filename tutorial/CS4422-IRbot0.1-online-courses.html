    YOUR TRAINING. YOUR TIMING.

    At the College of Professional Education, we understand that face-to-face learning doesn’t always fit your schedule. That’s why we offer over 350 online courses and 65 certificate programs to help you learn the skills needed to advance your career. Earn a certificate of completion for every course, and many programs also include vouchers to earn your professional certification. We offer two online formats to fit your life and your learning style.

    Monthly Courses

    Monthly online courses begin the middle of each month, and lessons release every Wednesday and Friday over the course of six weeks. There is no set time you need to be online to complete the lessons. Lessons include quizzes, assignments, discussion areas, and supplemental links to help you excel in your chosen topic. After passing your final exam with an 80% or higher, you will be mailed a certificate of completion.

    Self-Paced Courses

    Self-Paced courses contain the same lessons as our Monthly online courses. All lessons are immediately available once enrollment verification is complete, on each Wednesday by 5 p.m. PLEASE NOTE: There is no instructor support for self-paced courses. Certificates are printed the first business day of the month for all successful completions (final exam score of 80 or higher) in the month prior.
