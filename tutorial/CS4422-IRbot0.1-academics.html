    CYBR 3300 Mandatory Advising Documents

    The following documents are used in CYBR 3300 as part of the mandatory advising assignment:

    • Major Advising Checklist
    • General Education Advising Checklist
    • Advisors Affadavit
    • Students Plan of Study

    As part of that assignment, students must download and complete all documents, schedule an appointment with an advisor, and provide the documents to the advisor prior to their appointment for review.
