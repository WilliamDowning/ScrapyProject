    Records and Information Management

    Page last updated 1/11/2022
    UPDATE: To request Maintenance Services please see our forms page. For questions or concerns, please email records@kennesaw.edu.

    KSU's Records and Information Management (RIM) program aims to provide clear direction for establishing auditable methods of capturing, classifying, storing, retrieving, retaining, and disposing of records and information, to support KSU's Records and Information Management Policy.

    RIM works closely with KSU Archives to identify and acquire University records of enduring value for future generations of students, researchers, and historians.
