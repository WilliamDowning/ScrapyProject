SummerU is 35

35 years of learning, creativity, and fun!

Since 1987, the College of Professional Education has been home to summer camps that foster growth and exploration for Atlanta’s children. This year, SummerU is back and better than ever with a huge variety of day camps! This summer, there will be something for everyone: technology, art, STEM, theatre, culinary, and more!  Our full camp schedule is available now. 

 

With over 100 unique camp experiences, we have plenty of hands-on fun for everyone. Campers can discover a new hobby, learn a cool life skill, or express their creativity in a friendly and supportive environment. Our instructors are experts in their fields (some are even college professors!) and enjoy helping kids create, explore, and excel. Get ready for summer fun at KSU!
