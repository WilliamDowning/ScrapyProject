    Job & Internship Fairs

    Each semester, The Department of Career Planning and Development hosts job and internship fairs to help KSU students connect with potential employers. Information about upcoming fairs can be found in the Handshake platform.

    On-Campus Job Fairs

    Want to work on campus? Check out the On-Campus Job Fairs conveniently held on each campus to meet with departments hiring students like you!

    Accounting, Finance, and FinTech Job and Internship Fair

    If you've thought about working in Accounting, Finance, or FinTech - this is the fair for you! Explore internships, part-time, and full-time opportunities in these (and other related) industries.

    STEM Job, Co-op, and Internship Fair

    This fair is for students seeking any position in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math-related fields. You don't have to be a STEM major to find great opportunities at this fair! 

    Fall Job & Internship Fair (for all majors)

    There's something for everyone at this fair. Employers will represent a wide variety of industries, from healthcare to technology and human services to communications. All majors and class levels are encouraged to attend! 

    Graduate School Recruitment Fair

    If grad school might be in your future, don't miss this opportunity to explore MBA programs, law schools, med schools, and plenty of other graduate degree programs available across the state and region!

    Virtual Job & Internship Fair

    This fair connects students with the nation's top employers... all through Handshake! Employers can host 30-minute group sessions and 10-minute 1:1 sessions. Log in to Handshake to start signing up now.

    Check out our events calendar to find workshops and other opportunities to get prepared for each job fair!
