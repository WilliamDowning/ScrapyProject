Department of Dance: 
where imagination moves!

The Department of Dance at Kennesaw State University, located just north of Atlanta, is home to the largest collegiate dance program in Georgia, offering a Bachelor of Arts degree with concentrations in ballet and contemporary modern dance. Want to know more about majoring in Dance? Click here.
