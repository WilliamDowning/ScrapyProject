Announcements

  • The Department of Sports and Recreation is committed to mitigating the risk to COVID-19 exposure for our students, patrons, and staff. We've implemented safety guidelines for facilities, programs, and services to include increased cleaning protocols and an updated code of conduct for use of all recreation facilities. 

    Detailed information can be found on our reopening webpage. 

    Please call us at (470) 578-6913 or email us at sports_recreation@kennesaw.edu

    Additional University Information and Resources

    • All outdoor courts at the Student Recreation and Activities Center are closed for maintenance and resurfacing. Courts will remain closed for the Fall 2022 semester.
