          • Esports Progam

            Location:

            • Staff Office: Student Affairs Suite - Office 230C
              Team Practice Space: Located in the WSC Game Room

            The Esports Program at KSU, a collection of intercollegiate competitive teams, events for students, campus resources, and a growing social community all geared towards esports.

            Learn More about Esports Program

             

          • Marietta Activities Council

            Location: Student Affairs Suite - Office 230p

            From the Fall Festival to a casual Spa Day, they are determined to make the best of your college career! MAC is a student-run organization providing a college experience through social events in a fun, stress-free environment, while providing opportunities for growth in campus involvement, social networking, and leadership. 

            Learn More about the Marietta Activities Council

             

          • Student Government Association

            Location: Student Affairs Suite - Office 230h

            The purpose of the Student Government Association is to: REPRESENT the students of Kennesaw State University; SERVE as a liaison between students and members of the University community within the shared governance structure; actively strive to ENHANCE student success; FOSTER an environment of campus engagement; and ADVOCATE on behalf of the students’ welfare in accordance with KSU statutes, student codes of conduct, and the policies of the Board of Regents.

            Learn More about SGA

             

          • The Leadership Hub

            Location:

            • Staff Offices: Student Affairs Suite - Office 230g and 230j
            • The Leadership Hub: Student Affairs Suite - Room 230f

            Leadership is the intentional process of working together to create positive change.  This change may be as small as an interaction between two people or as large as the creation of new technology.

            Visit The Leadership Hub to receive individual coaching, student organization support, resources on leadership, and more. 

            Learn More about The Leadership Hub
          • Unity Center

            Locations:

            • Staff Office: Student Affairs Suite - Office 230e
            • The Unity Center: Room 271 and 272

            At the Unity Center, we focus on developing student leadership, creating connections, and producing programs that demonstrate intersectionality. 
             
            The Unity Center has two rooms on the second floor. Room 271 is the perfect place to do homework or chill between classes! It has an open-door policy from 9-5PM Monday through Friday, and there is a conference table, couches, two computers, and a microwave. Room 272 is a quieter room perfect for when you need some space to yourself to study, if you have an interview you need to do privately, or if you need space for any religious needs. Just ask one of the student assistants to open the office for you.
             
            Additionally, we have resources for women, LGTBQ students, minority students, and more! If you have any questions about what resources we offer, please ask a student assistant or visit Madison Link in room 230E.

            To learn what programs and events we have coming up, check OwlLife and follow us on Instagram @KSUUnityCenter

          Additional Offices and Resources

          • CARE Services and Food Pantry

            Location: Room 184

            A resource for all students, CARE offers support through the Food Pantry, Emergency Assistance Program, and other resources.

            Learn More about CARE Services
          • Career Planning and Development

            Location: Suite 160

            Dedicated to helping students and alumni begin their career path, the Department of Career Planning and Development has many resources to support the job, internship and co-op search process. 

            Learn More about Career Planning and Development
          • Center for Young Adult Addiction and Recovery (CYAAR)

            Location: Suite 260

            CYAAR supports and encourages recovery and wellness in an abstinence-hostile social environment. By providing recovery-based programs, a peer recovery community, and education to help those impacted by addiction, the Center changes lives, connects students to the community, and lays a solid foundation for transformational recovery.

            Learn More about CYAAR
          • Counseling and Psychological Services

            Location: Suite 170

            Counseling and Psychological Services provides a wide range of service, including psychoeducational workshops aimed at helping students develop skills for managing the stress of college, group counseling focused on helping student better manage emotions and navigate their relationships, individual counseling for a wide range of mental wellness concerns, as well as psychiatric medication evaluation and treatment.

            Learn More about CPS
          • Department of Dance

            The Wilson Student Center is home to the state-or-the-art Dance Theater, home of KSU's Department of Dance. 

            Learn More about the Department of Dance
          • Divison of Diverse and Inclusive Excellence

            The Division of Diverse and Inclusive Excellence seeks to create a culture of inclusion at Kennesaw State University where all members of the community can achieve excellence, succeed, and thrive.

            Phone: (470) 578-2614
            Fax: (470) 578-9058
            E-mail: ksudiverse@kennesaw.edu

            Learn More about the Division of Diverse and Inclusive Excellence
          • Health Promotion and Wellness

            Location: Suite 260

            To support a healthy learning environment for students, Health Promotion and Wellness offers a variety of services and programs at a free or discounted cost. The programs and services offered support students’ overall wellness through health education workshops and events including, but not limited to, stress management programs, nutrition consultations and workshops, risk reduction education, CPR certification, and more.

            Learn More about HPW
          • Military and Veteran Services

            Location: Suite 185

            KSU Military & Veteran Services serves as the designated point of contact that assists service members, veterans, and dependents with academic and financial advising as it relates to Tuition Assistance and/or VA educational benefits. Military & Veteran Services provides access to disability counseling and job search activities via on-campus and off-campus referrals to the appropriate departments and/or agencies.Through benefits and Tuition Assistance counseling, academic and financial advising, referral services to on and off-campus stakeholders, and outreach programs, our Military and Veteran Services team seeks to provide an inclusive and educational environment that fosters student success and achievement. Whether you are a new or transferring student, we will help guide you to the resources you need to make the most of your experience at KSU, while setting you up for success beyond the classroom.

            Learn More about MVS
          • Office of Student Advocacy

            Location: Student Affairs Suite - Office 230k

            Student Advocacy serves as a central support to all KSU students. We help students manage life challenges, and to overcome barriers that may impede success.

            Need to Report an Issue? Review the steps here.

            Learn More about Student Advocacy
          • Owl Swap Clothing Closet

            Location: Room 187

            OwlSwap Closet is a resource that provides all KSU students, faculty, and staff with free access to professional attire for academic and employment opportunities as well as everyday clothing. Not only does it give everyone a chance to acquire new-to-you clothing, but it also encourages sustainable consumption.

            Shop online @owlswapcloset on Instagram, or visit an in-person location. 

            Learn More about OwlSwap Closet

             

          • Student Activities

            Location: Room 230n

            Student Activities supports all 270+ Registered Student Organizations, including all Student Media publications. We coordinate University-wide activities & programs such as Week of Welcome and Homecoming, as well as monthly programs. The Kennesaw Activities Board (KAB) and Marietta Activities Council (MAC) are advised by Student Activities staff.

            For questions specific to Marietta Campus Student Affairs, visit us in the Student Affairs Suite 230 or email MariettaSTA@kennesaw.edu

            Learn More about Student Activities
          • Student Disability Services

            Location: Suite 160

            Student Disability Services (SDS) is the first contact for students with disabilities to arrange accommodations and locate campus and community resources. To access accommodations, students must first connect with SDS and provide the appropriate documentation as outlined by the Board of Regents of the University System of Georgia.
