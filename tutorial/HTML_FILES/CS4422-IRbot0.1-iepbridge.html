    Intensive English Bridge Program

    The Intensive English Bridge Program at Kennesaw State University is designed for students looking to receive their graduate degree from a university in the United States, but do not yet meet the language requirements.

    Students will:

    • Enroll full-time in our Intensive English Program
    • Complete the program and meet the English language requirements
    • Enroll as a graduate student at KSU
