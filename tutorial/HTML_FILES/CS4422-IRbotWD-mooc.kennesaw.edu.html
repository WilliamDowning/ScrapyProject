The Office of Technology Enhanced Learning is excited to provide access and affordability to the KSU community via the Coursera/KSU/USG partnership.

All USG sponsored MOOCs, designed and delivered by KSU faculty, are available at no charge to KSU students, faculty and staff via their NetID.

Differentiate yourself and your KSU learning experience with one of the most powerful micro-credentials offered.

MOOCs at Kennesaw State University

MOOCs at Kennesaw State are faculty driven initiatives designed to increase access to education and highlight KSU's centers of excellence. The goal is to offer educational pathways that invite the regional, national, and international community to join in a learning experience. The Open Courses at KSU have the potential to lower program costs and increase access to education while shortening degree completion time. In accordance with Complete College Georgia, MOOCs will also target adult learners and those from rural areas seeking to expand their educational opportunities. Open courses will offer levels of participation ranging from general community interest to professional learning or certification. Several courses will offer options for qualified students to convert their MOOC activities to transfer credit.
