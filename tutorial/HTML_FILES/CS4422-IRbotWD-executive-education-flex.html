Welcome to Executive Education Flex

Busy working professionals need flexible learning options. Introducing KSU Executive Education Flex. 100% online, anytime, anywhere. Flexible learning on your schedule from your laptop, tablet, or smartphone. Our courses are available on laptops, tablets, or smartphones giving you the flexibility to learn on your schedule.

The Learn-Do-Inspire behavior-centered learning platform from Rali, KSU Coles College of Business online learning partner, allows you to learn through game-ified play. Our online learning programs use the latest techniques in microlearning. Conquer quests, tackle challenges, earn points. Access useful knowledge just when you need to succeed.

Why Choose Executive Education Flex?

  • Fun, flexible online learning
  • Innovative, award-winning faculty and instructors
  • Quick implementation of key concepts and action plans
  • Learn the secret to improve sales, increase revenue, and foster a growth-mindset workplace
