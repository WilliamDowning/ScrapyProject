Find Your Wings

There is no better choice for graduate education than Kennesaw State University. One of the world’s leading research universities, KSU offers top-ranked graduate programs across all disciplines, a welcoming community for international students and great value and affordability.

Visit Campus

You’ve got a big decision to make. See for yourself why KSU is the ideal choice. Our in-person campus tours are designed specifically for students and their families. Led by a tour guide, the campus tour highlights academic programs, housing, dining and admissions. You can choose to visit both the Kennesaw and Marietta campuses or the campus that houses your area of academic interest.
