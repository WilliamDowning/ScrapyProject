Active Minds Send Silence Packing®
Division of Student Affairs, Counseling and Psychological Services, and the Wellbeing@KSU collaborative is honored to bring the Active Minds Send Silence Packing®  exhibit to KSU. For more than a decade, Active Minds’ acclaimed Send Silence Packing® exhibit has traveled the country to end the silence that surrounds mental health and suicide and connected visitors with resources for support and action. 
The all-day exhibit includes personal stories from individuals who have lost a loved one to suicide and is designed to raise awareness about the impact of suicide, connect individuals to mental health resources, and inspire action for suicide prevention. Passersby are invited to walk among the Send Silence Packing backpacks to see the photographs and read the stories attached to them. Many of the backpacks were donated by families who lost a loved one to suicide.
If you are unable to attend the exhibit in person, you can explore the virtual program “Behind the Backpacks” by visiting https://www.activeminds.org/behind-the-backpacks/
