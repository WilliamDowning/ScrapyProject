We know learning how to apply for college can be overwhelming, and we want to help make this easier for students. Our college admissions counselors are available to answer any questions you might have during the application and admission process. You can contact the Office of Undergraduate Admissions by phone, e-mail, postal, or other delivery services. Visit us in person by using the campus map and driving directions in the link below.
