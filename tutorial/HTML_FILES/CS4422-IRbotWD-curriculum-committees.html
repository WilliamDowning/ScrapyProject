    Undergraduate Policies and Curriculum Committee

    Click here for the UPCC Docket 

    A university standing committee of the Faculty Senate, the UPCC evaluates new undergraduate program proposals and proposed changes to the undergraduate curriculum for consistency with university academic policies and goals.  The UPCC forwards to the Provost those proposals it recommends for approval. This body recommends or reviews changes in undergraduate policies and procedures and directs its recommendations to the  Faculty Senate Executive Committee and the Provost/AVPAA. As needed, the committee also makes recommendations regarding the undergraduate curriculum development and review process.
