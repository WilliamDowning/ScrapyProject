    Welcome!

    Hello! Welcome to the home of the Kennesaw State University College Panhellenic Council (CPC)! As the governing body of our eight Panhellenic sororities, we strive to promote sisterhood through our four pillars; leadership, scholarship, community and service. Utilize our website to find information like; chapter spotlights, our blog, and all things for Panhellenic sorority recruitment. 

    We are so happy to welcome 358 women into our community this fall! We hope you are enjoying your time at Kennesaw as well as your new member period!
