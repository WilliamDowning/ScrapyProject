The KSU African American Male Initiative (AAMI) program is a minority-male enrichment program that provides academic, leadership, and professional opportunities to KSU students. The unique set of programming offered through AAMI focuses on the inspirational tenet of brotherhood and can include: regional leadership conferences, guest lectures, peer & professional mentorship, social outings, leadership workshops and more. AAMI programming empowers black men to achieve academic and personal excellence so that they may become leaders in their families and communities.
