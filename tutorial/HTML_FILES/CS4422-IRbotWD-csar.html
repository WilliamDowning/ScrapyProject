    In Bill’s roles as Chief Analytics Officer for both Teradata and The International Institute For Analytics, he was lucky enough to meet with analytics executives in hundreds of large companies around the world. In this webcast, he interviews many of the executives and professionals he’s met to learn about how their careers unfolded, what skills they look for when hiring, what trends they think are coming next, and more! If you want an inside view into what some of today’s top data science names are thinking and talking about, this series is for you!
