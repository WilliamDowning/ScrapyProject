The Department of Health Promotion and Physical Education (HPE) offers undergraduate degrees in Integrated Health Science, Public Health Education, and P-12 Health and Physical Education. The Department also offers minors in both Coaching and Public Health which are open to any degree-seeking undergraduate student at KSU. The department serves the campus community through an extensive array of Instructional Physical Activity (IPAP) and Wellness courses.
