    • Welcome to Owl Advising

      Owl Advising is the primary advising home for students who are:

      • Undeclared or in a Focus Area
      • Dual Enrollment Students
      • Students Exploring a New Major
      • Students under 30 credit hours who are Academically At Risk
      • New Students Requiring Learning Support

      Owl Advising is also a secondary advising resource for those students who have a declared major but need assistance general education requirements or university policies.

      Owl Advising prides itself on being a welcoming office that supports all students that come through its doors.
