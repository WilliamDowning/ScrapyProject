Connect With Us

Visit us in the Kennesaw Campus Carmichael Student Center, Suite 267. Call us at 470-578-6700, or email us at dsl@kennesaw.edu.

If you have any serious questions or concerns about your experience at Kennesaw State, please voice them at concern.kennesaw.edu.

The Department of Student Leadership and Service, as part of the Division of Student Affairs, is dedicated to the success of our students, both academically & personally.
