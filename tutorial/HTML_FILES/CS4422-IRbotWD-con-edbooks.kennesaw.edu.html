Professional Education Online Textbook Ordering Information

All Professional Education Book Orders will be available for UPS ground shipping or pickup at one of 3 available locations. You will have two weeks from the date your order is processed to pick up your initial order or back order. After that time your order will be cancelled and the materials restocked.

Orders are typically filled withing 1-2 business days. Items not in stock will be placed on back-order and filled as soon as they are available. You should receive an email notification when your order has processed and is shipping or that it has been processed and is ready for pickup.

Technology Purchases: All of the merchandise available in our technology store, OwlTEC, is available to Professional Education students. However, there are versions of some software that are only available to fully enrolled KSU students. Please see an OwlTEC representative or contact them at 470.578.6198 for assistance.  The OwlTEC store is located on the main Kennesaw Campus, just inside the Student Center.


 Professional Education Bookstore Refund Policies

All Refunds require that you present us your original sales receipt. Regular Professional Education classes are returnable up to 3 days after the class starts. After that the purchase is non refundable. For One Day Classes, the book is only refundable prior to the class starting. If the class has already started, the book is considered non-refundable.
