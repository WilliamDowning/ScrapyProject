​Live Where the Owls Live

Movies about college aren’t made about students who live at home and commute to class! Here are five reasons why you should live where the Owls live to get the complete college experience.

Get the Grades

Students who live on campus have higher cumulative GPAs than students who live off campus.

Graduate on Time

Students who live on campus are more likely to graduate on time compared to students who don't.

KSU Is Right Outside Your Door

You are steps away from class, academic resources, nationally ranked dining options, student recreation areas and more.

Get Involved

Living on campus provides you with easy access to the 300+ student organizations, student activities and thousands of events that are a huge part of your college experience.

24/7 Support

Our team of 150 student and professional staff members are here for you 24 hours a day, seven days a week, 365 days a year. Whether you get locked out of your room or need someone to talk to, we’re here for you.

Ready to Call KSU Your Home?

Get the most out of your time at Kennesaw State University by living on-campus, where everything you need to succeed is just outside your door.
