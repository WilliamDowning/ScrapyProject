Join us Saturday, October 15, 2022, 6 p.m. - 9 p.m., in the Cobb Energy Performing Arts Centre. 

We look forward to welcoming you to the 2022 ArtsKSU Gala! This year's Gala will also celebrate the Investiture of KSU President Kathy Schwaig. 

You will be entertained and inspired by visual artists from the School of Art and Design, dancers from the Department of Dance, musicians from the Bailey School of Music, and musical theatre performances from the Department of Theatre and Performance Studies.

Enjoy a cash bar and hors d'oeuvres, followed by dinner featuring your entre selection. Your meal will end on a sweet note with a gourmet dessert. Bid on silent auction items throughout the evening and participate in our exciting live auction. 

Proceeds will benefit annual student scholarships in the College of the Arts at KSU.

Parking: Convenient, covered parking is available in the Cobb Energy Performing Arts Centre's garage for $15. (Please note: Only cards are accepted for parking service , no cash.)
