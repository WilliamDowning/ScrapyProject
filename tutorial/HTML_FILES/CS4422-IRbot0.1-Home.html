Your Next Career Move Starts Here

 

As you look ahead to 2020, what goals do you have for your career? Maybe you’re preparing for your next promotion. Or perhaps you’ve decided to go in a new direction and change careers. Or maybe you want to explore your options and find the best career path for you. Whatever your goals may be, the College of Professional Education (CPE) is here to help you reach them.

Join us on January 9 for our Open House, where we’ll have free info sessions on skills-based certificate courses, along with:

  • Discounts on course enrollment fees, including additional savings for those who enroll in a certificate course during the event. See discount details below.
  • Career advice workshops
  • *New for January 2020* Complimentary professional headshots for registered attendees (headshots will be available on a first come first serve basis)
  • Course Information tables to answer your questions throughout the event
  • Light refreshments

This is your opportunity to speak with course instructors and CPE staff, ask questions, and find your path to a rewarding career.

Open House Enrollment Discount

All Open House attendees will be eligible to save 10% on course registration fees. Restrictions apply; see below. Save $500 on Culinary Apprenticeship and Medical Assisting. Expires Thursday, January 23 @11:59PM.

Enroll in your course the night of Open House for additional savings! Attendees who enroll during the event in person at our registration window on January 9 from 6-8:30 p.m. will save 20% on course registration fees. Restrictions apply; see below. Save $1000 on Culinary Apprenticeship and Medical Assisting. The 20% discount cannot be used on OLLI, Online, or Personal Enrichment courses.

Cannot be combined with any other discount (including Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act, MyCAA, VA education benefits or military discount). The 10% discount can be applied when registering online by entering code and selecting the Apply Promo Code button during checkout. Discounts are not valid on conferences held at the KSU Center, courses offered in conjunction with Kennesaw State University, or the Pedorthist Certificate.

Note: You must call to register for the following certificate courses: Certified Nursing Assistant, Cisco CCNA, Culinary Apprenticeship, Information Systems Security Professional (ISSP), Medical Assisting, Medical Billing and Coding Specialist, and Medical Interpreter. You can also register in person at our Registration desk inside the KSU Center.
