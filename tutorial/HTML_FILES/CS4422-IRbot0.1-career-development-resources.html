    CAREER ADVISING

    Career Advising at KSU Professional Education is designed to provide students with the most current local data on wages, industry growth, job postings, and associated skills, education and training. 

    We offer complimentary career advising sessions to prospective, current and past students as an opportunity to discuss advancement opportunities within your current field or careers pathways that may align well with your interests, goals and skills, including what short and long-term plans might look like for working toward your personal and professional goals.

    S2S is offered through our partnership with Accenture and is designed to help individuals build the skills and confidence they need to make smart career choices and find and succeed in employment.

    The program consists of dozens of interactive modules on topics including Career Exploration, Resume and Cover Letter Writing, Professional Online Presence, Interviewing Skills and skills to help you succeed at the workplace.

    Handshake is an all-in-one career management system for our alumni which includes a job board, document/resource library and communication system. Alumni can search and apply for jobs in Handshake and will have their resumes reviewed by a staff member. 

    All CPE students who successfully complete a certificate program are eligible to request a Handshake account.
