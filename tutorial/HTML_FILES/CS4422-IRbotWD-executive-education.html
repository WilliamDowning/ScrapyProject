The Executive Education Center at the Coles College of Business is dedicated to helping you and your organization succeed. As one of the country’s most innovative universities in teaching and learning, we design executive development programs using best practices in program customization, technology-enhanced learning and on-the-job application. Our classroom-based certificate programs and our digital badge mobile-learning programs are designed to help you reach your fullest potential as leaders, innovators and change-makers.

Upcoming Certificate Programs and Workshops

Executive Leadership Program for Managers and Directors - Next Cohort begins August 18, 2022

Finally, a leadership development certificate program that integrates the best practices of gamified mobile-learning and classroom action-learning. The Michael J. Coles College of Business now offers an Executive Leadership Program for Managers and Directors designed for busy professionals who want to take their leadership skills, and their careers, to the next level. Learn how to master the 8 leader roles and the 24 leadership skills.

Creative Problem Solving Foundations - Single Day Workshop  - September 9, 2022

Creative Problem Solving is a process successful leaders and teams use to find solutions to difficult challenges. Industry leaders identify it as a Top 5 “in-demand” job skill along with Innovation and Creative Thinking. In this hands-on workshop you will experience the Creative Problem Solving (CPS) process and explore tools to create breakthrough ideas that add value and drive revenue.

Lean Six Sigma Green Belt Program - Next Cohort - September 12, 13, 19 and 20, 2022

Lean Six Sigma Green Belt Program: Designed for those with little or no prior experience in Continuous Improvement, this program is an introduction to the tools and techniques of Lean and the DMAIC methodology of Six Sigma. DMAIC (Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve and Control) refers to a data-driven problem-solving approach that can be applied across all industries. Lean refers to the waste reduction tools and principles made famous by Toyota. LSS Green Belt participants learn the framework for solving problems and improving processes in a variety of hands-on projects and activities.  During the program, participants will complete at least 3 LSS projects and leave the program with the tools needed to solve real world problems.

Certified Mergers & Acquisitions Professional (CMAP) Program- Next Cohort begins October 11, 2022

A popular certificate program in partnership with the M&A Source. Larry Stevens, CPA, after a long and distinguished career with PwC leads the program. Topics include M&A strategy, process planning, due diligence, financial analysis, transaction valuation, deal structuring and negotiations.

Executive Certificate in Business Strategy: The Coles Mini MBA Program - Next cohort begins –  January 25, 2023

The upcoming Coles Mini MBA program offered by the Michael J. Coles College of Business at Kennesaw State University is cohort-based, 100% synchronous online (via Zoom) designed to improve your business knowledge and acumen with weekly sessions (6-9 PM).

The topics include innovation, strategy, finance, marketing, professional sales, supply chain/operations, human resources, and change management. At the end of the program, you will receive an Executive Certificate in Business Strategy.

Executive Education FLEX

Busy working professionals need flexible learning options. Introducing KSU Executive Education Flex. 100% online, anytime, anywhere. Flexible learning on your schedule from your laptop, tablet, or smartphone. Our courses are available on laptops, tablets, or smartphones giving you the flexibility to learn on your schedule.
