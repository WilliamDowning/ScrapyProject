Thank You for Your Interest in Kennesaw State University!

At KSU, we put our students first in everything we do all 42,000+ of them and this starts from day one. When you enroll at Kennesaw State, you learn from our world-class faculty and gain academic support from in-the-know advisors. You’ll find your community by becoming involved in one or more of our 300+ student organizations, living in one of our on-campus residence halls and refueling at one of our award-winning campus dining options. And you’ll find your spirit cheering for one of our 18 NCAA Division I sports teams.

FIND YOUR PATH TO SUCCESS

Amazing things happen here, and it’s not by coincidence. Students at KSU support one another, and you will find a wealth of resources along with the encouragement of knowledgeable faculty to help you achieve your goals.

 

HELPING YOU REACH, ACHIEVE AND THRIVE

Graduating from high school and beginning college is a big leap. Students in KSU’s Achieve Atlanta, Reach GA, and Thrive (HOPE) Scholars Programs gain a network of campus support, build community and help with the transition into college life. These scholarship-focused communities help you engage on and off-campus, gain life skills, prepare for your future career, maintain your GPA, and stay on course to graduate.

RESOURCES FOR HISPANIC/LATINX STUDENTS

KSU is dedicated to increasing the retention, progression, and graduation rates for all Hispanic/Latinx students with a proven, established program. The Recruitment, Retention, & Progression to Graduation (RRPG) Program for Hispanic and Latino Students provides dedicated cultural resources, academic support, mentorship, special assistance, and financial aid access uniquely tailored to the needs of this student population.

INTERNSHIPS & CO-OPS

Internships and co-ops help you make the most of your KSU experience by allowing you the opportunity to connect with industry professionals and gain valuable work experience. These experiences help build your resume and prepare you for your future career. 

LEADERSHIP OPPORTUNITIES

KSU offers a wide variety of programs and services to assist you in developing your leadership potential to make an impact on our campuses and create positive change in the world.

CAREER PLANNING STARTS FROM YEAR ONE

KSU believes engaging experiences help set the stage for your professional advancement. That’s why we encourage you to participate in resume-building undergraduate research and service-learning opportunities that expand beyond the classroom. Your career advisor can help you select a major that fits your talents, assist you in exploring career opportunities and prepare you for job interviews that lead to internships, co-ops and ultimately your dream care.

Are You Ready?

Our campuses radiate energy and excitement — where students strive to be more, do more, and become more. We work hand in hand to make big, bold promises to discover our potential and defy expectations every single day.

Owls are ready to put in the work, rise to the challenge and define their legacies. It’s the limitless possibility supported by the resources and mentors that will help you find your purpose and dare to climb higher.
