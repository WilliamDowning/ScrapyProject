Kennesaw State University Open House

You are invited to attend Kennesaw State University's Open House!

Open House offers you the opportunity to learn more about your major and your desired field of study. You will also receive information on admission requirements and financial aid, and you will have the chance to hear from current KSU students.

5 reasons to attend KSU’s Open House

  • Experience the Campus "Fit"

    You can’t judge a college’s atmosphere by reading a brochure or looking at a website. You have to visit in person. Walk the campus and see the facilities. Many current students said they decided to apply because Kennesaw State just “felt” right.

  • Check out Housing

    Explore your housing options — an in-person visit makes planning what to bring to your future home a whole lot easier. You’ll also see how living on campus provides easy access to resources, activities and events.

  • Delicious Food!

    Enjoy a complimentary lunch in our award-winning dining facilities provided for up to three visitors.

  • Explore Scholarships and Aid

    Figuring out financial aid can be a challenge. At Open House, financial aid counselors will answer questions and help you understand the types of aid, grants and scholarships available. Come learn about FAFSA, HOPE, student work and much more.

  • Learn about Majors and Programs

    You can pick from academic sessions based on KSU’s colleges. You will get a feel for what KSU courses and faculty are like, and you can ask questions about degrees or academic opportunities.

Visit our Campus!

Visiting campus is a great opportunity to explore KSU and learn about Owl Nation.

Kennesaw State is a student-centered, research-driven university with a vibrant campus culture and an entrepreneurial spirit. As Owls, we are guided by a purpose and, no matter how we change, grow, or evolve, we never forget who we are. Welcome to Owl Nation!
