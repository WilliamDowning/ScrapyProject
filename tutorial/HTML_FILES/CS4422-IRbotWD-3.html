PARKING AND DIRECTIONS
KSU CONVOCATION CENTER
Sports: Volleyball, Men's Basketball & Women's Basketball

Directions From I-75: Exit 271 / Chastain Road and head west on Chastain Road. Turn right at the first traffic light (Frey Road). At the first traffic light turn left into the East Parking Deck.

General Parking: General parking is available at the East Deck parking garage and East Lot. Please obey all posted campus parking information.

Handicapped Parking: Designated parking for those with special needs is available in all campus parking areas. Patrons must display the required handicapped-parking permit.


STILLWELL STADIUM & BAILEY PARK

Sports: Baseball at Stillwell Stadium, Softball at Bailey Park

Directions From I-75: Exit 271 / Chastain Road and head west on Chastain Road. Turn right at the first traffic light onto Frey Road. At the first traffic light turn left into the East Parking Deck.

General Parking: General parking is available in the East Deck parking garage and is a first come first served basis. Please obey all posted campus parking information.

Handicapped Parking: Drop off is available for those with special needs, however handicapped parking is limited adjacent to the fields.


FIFTH THIRD BANK STADIUM

Sports: Football, Soccer, Lacrosse (Football-specific information can be found here)

Directions From I-75: Exit 271 / Chastain Road and head east on Chastain Road. Turn right and head south on Busbee Drive (the first red light) and the stadium will be at the end of Busbee Drive.

General Parking: General parking is available in the CCT lot, and is a first come first served basis.

CCT Lot Address for GPS: 
3019 George Busbee Parkway NW
Kennesaw, GA 30144

Handicapped Parking: Drop off is available for those with special needs, however handicapped parking is limited adjacent to the Stadium entrance on the east side of the stadium.


BETTY SIEGEL TENNIS COURTS

Sports: Tennis

Directions From I-75: Exit 271 / Chastain Road and head west on Chastain Road. Turn right at the first traffic light onto Frey Road. At the first traffic light turn left into the East Parking Deck.

General Parking: General parking is available in the East Deck parking garage and is a first come first served basis. Please obey all posted campus parking information.
Handicapped Parking: Drop off is available for those with special needs, however handicapped parking is limited adjacent to the fields.
