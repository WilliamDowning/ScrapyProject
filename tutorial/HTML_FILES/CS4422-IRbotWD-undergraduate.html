Find your Wings

Kennesaw State University empowers students to seek, strive and succeed in academics and beyond. In addition to offering more than 180 degree and certificate programs in 11 colleges, KSU leads in innovative teaching and learning and boasts two beautiful campuses with nearly 43,000 students. Owl Nation is home to vibrant campus cultures, close enough to Atlanta for students to explore exciting events, fun activities, and coveted career opportunities. We’re excited that you’ve chosen KSU as your academic doorway to reach your future potential and purpose.     
 
Our number one priority at KSU is our students and their success. KSU offers majors in STEM fields, nursing, arts, cybersecurity, business, and many more. Enjoy apartment-style residences, nationally ranked dining facilities, and supportive campus life just north of Atlanta. Are you ready to soar?  
At KSU, you’ll Find Your Wings!

Visit Campus

You’ve got a big decision. See for yourself why KSU is the ideal choice. Our in-person campus tours are designed specifically for students and their families. Led by a tour guide, the campus tour highlights academic programs, housing, dining, and admissions. You can choose to visit both the Kennesaw and Marietta campuses or the campus that houses your area of academic interest.
