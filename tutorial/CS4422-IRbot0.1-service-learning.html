  • Interested in Teaching a Service-Learning Course?

    You can find the USG Service-Learning Handbook here. 

    Please contact Ryan Keesee (rkeesee@kennesaw.edu) with any additional questions regarding service-learning with the institution or resources available. 

  • Pedagogy

    Please visit the Center for Excellence in Teaching and Learning (CETL) page dedicated to Quality Enhancement Plan resources. 

  • FAQ

    1.     How will I find community partners for my course?

    Once you've filled out the Service-Learning Interest Form, SLS will check its database of community partners interested in S-L collaboration to see if their available projects align with the goals of your course.

    2.     How will I know if community partners have opportunities to collaborate available?

    SLS maintains a database of community partners interested in S-L and the projects that they would like to collaborate on.

    3.     How can I align SL goals with my CLO?

    Please check out CETL's webpage for more information on S-L pedagogy.

  • Resources/Forms

    Use this checklist as a quick reference before starting your service-learning course.

    Here are some additional forms and templates helpful for S-L courses.

    • S-L release form
    • Letter of intent template
    • Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) template
  • College Liaisons

     Each KSU College has an assigned Liaison to provide support to instructors. 

    Contact Ryan Keesee (rkeesee@kennesaw.edu) to be connected with your Liaison.
