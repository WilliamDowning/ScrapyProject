    "The Marching Owls" Alumni Society

    • Marielle Matthews - President

      Shelby Marielle Matthews is a Georgia native, born and raised in the suburban Chattanooga area, and later, Neenah, Wisconsin. Marielle is a graduate of Kennesaw State University with a Bachelors of Music in Music Education with a concentration in voice, where she is a former officer and member of both Sigma Alpha Iota and the American Choral Director's Association. Marielle recently graduated with her Masters degree in Music Education from the University of Georgia.

      Marielle has taught private piano and voice lessons for elementary through high school aged students in the styles of musical theater, pop, folk, country, classical, and modern repertoire, and is currently the Choral Director at Creekland Middle School in Canton, GA for grades 6-8.

      Marielle proudly served "The Marching Owls" as their Feature Twirler during their 2017 season and she is also a former member of the 2014-2015 Kennesaw State University Dance Team; her choreography has been used by high school dance and theater programs around the country. Marielle is currently the Feature Twirler Coach for "The Marching Owls" and President of "The Marching Owls" Alumni Society at Kennesaw State University. She was recently inducted as an Honorary Member of Kappa Kappa Psi and is the current Treasurer for GA-ACDA.

      Linkedin: https://www.linkedin.com/in/s-marielle-matthews/

    • Jacob Morgan - Vice President

      Jacob Morgan is an accomplished young music educator based in McDonough, Georgia. Under the direction of Mr. Morgan, the Union Grove Middle School (UGMS) Band Program has increased the enrollment of the band program to its largest size in program history and has consistently earned Superior Ratings at Large Group Performance Evaluation. While serving as Director of Bands at UGMS, Mr. Morgan was also elected to serve as the Band Chair for District VI of the Georgia Music Educators Association (GMEA), developed the Henry County Unit Plans, Learning Progressions, and Learning Targets for Beginning, Intermediate, and Advanced Middle School Band, presented professional development sessions to other teachers in Henry County Schools, and has served as a Mentor to new band directors in the county. Mr. Morgan has also hosted and organized many different GMEA events, including District Band Auditions and Allstate Band Auditions. Mr. Morgan is a graduate of Kennesaw State University (KSU) with a Bachelors in Music Education and also serves as the Vice President of “The Marching Owls” Alumni Society of Kennesaw State University.

      Linkedin: www.linkedin.com/in/jacob-morgan-b1797b212 

    • Rachel Ray - Secretary

      Rachel Ray graduated from Kennesaw State University in 2020 with a Bachelor of Science in Public Relations. With a passion for education, Rachel currently supports students in the Atlanta area as a Regional Admissions Counselor for Augusta University. Rachel began her higher education journey at KSU as a tour guide, social media marketing intern, and communications assistant for Enrollment Services.

      Rachel served as a sister of Tau Beta Sigma, National Honorary Band Service Sorority and a Trumpet player in “The Marching Owls” and Basketball Band from 2017-2020 and is thrilled to keep ties with her Alma Mater as the Secretary of “The Marching Owls” Alumni Society.

      Linkedin: https://www.linkedin.com/in/rachelindiaray/ 

     

    "The Marching Owls" Alumni Society

    Mission

    “The Marching Owls” Alumni Society (MOAS) was officially chartered in 2021 in order to support the overall advancement of “The Marching Owls” and contribute to the development of “The Marching Owls” Alumni community.

    "The Marching Owls" Alumni Band Day

    MOAS will host fundraising opportunities, tailgates, and at least two events each year, including “The Marching Owls” Alumni Band Day. Former members will be invited back to perform on the field during a Kennesaw State University football game and reconnect with “The Marching Owls” family.
