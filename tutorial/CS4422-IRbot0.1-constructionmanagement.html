Why choose a degree in Construction Management at KSU?

  • $91,370: current median pay for Construction Managers
  • Job Outlook: 44,800 new jobs expected by 2026
  • Job Placement: Advisory Board is actively engaged in hiring KSU students and graduates
  • Scholarships: CM majors have the most available and are awarded the highest amount at KSU
  • Award-winning student organizations and competition teams
