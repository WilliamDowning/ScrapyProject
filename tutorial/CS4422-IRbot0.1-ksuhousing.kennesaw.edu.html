    Waitlist Information

    We are now on a waitlist for the following populations:

    • First-Year Females on the Kennesaw Campus
    • First-Year Females on the Marietta Campus
    • First-Year Males on the Kennesaw Campus
    • First-Year Males on the Marietta Campus
    • Upper Class Females on the Kennesaw Campus
    • Upper Class Females on the Marietta Campus
    • Upper Class Males on the Kennesaw Campus
    • Upper Class Males on the Marietta Campus

    Here are the three steps you need to take if you wish to add yourself to this waitlist.

    1. Visit the Housing Portal to add yourself to the waitlist. Once you sign your License Agreement in Step 1 of the Housing Application you will see “Roommate and Room Selection” appear in the gold menu bar of the Housing Portal. You can click here to add yourself to a waitlist. First-Year students will only be able to see a waitlist based on their classification, campus and gender. Upper Class students will be able to see both the Kennesaw and Marietta Upper Class waitlists but can only add themselves to one. Please remember that we do not place students on a waitlist; you must place yourself if you wish to be added to the waitlist for your population. 
    2. Take note of what number you are on the waitlist. Our system will automatically provide a number to you to let you know how many people are in front of you on the waitlist. You can check your place on the waitlist any time by returning to the front page of the Housing Portal.
    3. Be prepared to receive a call and/or an email at your KSU student email address with an on-campus housing offer. Each summer, we place hundreds of students from the waitlist into on-campus housing. Make sure you have a voicemail box that can receive voicemails so we can get in touch with you when it is your turn to receive a room. You will have 48 hours to accept a room booking before we offer the assignment to the next person on the waitlist. 

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • How do I join the waitlist?
      Once you sign your License Agreement in Step 1 of the Housing Application you will see “Roommate and Room Selection” appear in the gold menu bar of the Housing Portal. You can click here to add yourself to a waitlist.
      • First-Year Students: You will only see one waitlist based on your classification, campus and gender.
      • Upper Class Students: You must place yourself on either the Kennesaw or Marietta Campus waitlist. You cannot add yourself to both.
    • When will you stop adding students to the waitlist?
      We will place students from the waitlist throughout the summer until Friday, July 29. At that time, we will pause placements until after Move-In, and begin placing again starting on August 15. We encourage you to be patient as we move through cancellations.  
    • Will I receive a refund for the $200 application initiation fee I paid during Step 1 since I did not get housing?
      If you are a First-Year or new KSU student, you paid a $200 non-refundable application initiation fee at the start of the Housing Application. That non-refundable fee is not a deposit and does not guarantee students a room. 
    • I do not want to add myself to the waitlist. How do I cancel my Housing Application?
      If you are no longer interested in living on campus and do not wish to place yourself on a waitlist, please complete this form to cancel your 2022-23 Housing Application. If, after canceling, you change your mind, you will need to reapply for housing and add yourself to the end of the waitlist.
    • How do I break up my Roommate Pair? (First-Year Students Only)

      If you are the Group Leader, follow these steps:

      1. Log in to the Housing Portal and click on “Roommate & Room Selection” in the gold menu bar.
      2. Click “Continue” to enter this portion of the application.
      3. Navigate to the “Roommate Pair Management” page.
      4. Click the gold “Delete Group” button to leave your group and search for rooms on your own. Please note: if you leave a group, we cannot help you re-create or re-join your group.
      5. To view your individual time ticket if you have not started Room Selection, click “2022-23 Application” tab in the gold menu. You will see a green box with your time ticket.

      If you are a Group Member, follow these steps: (You only need to leave a group if the Group Leader has not deleted the group.)

      1. Log in to the Housing Portal and click on “Roommate & Room Selection” in the gold menu bar.
      2. Click “Continue” to enter this portion of the application.
      3. Navigate to the “Roommate Pair Management” page.
      4. Click the gold “Leave This Pair” button to leave your group and search for rooms on your own. Please note: if you leave a group, we cannot help you re-create or re-join your group.
      5. To view your individual time ticket if you have not started Room Selection, click “2022-23 Application” tab in the gold menu. You will see a green box with your time ticket.
    • When will you stop placing students from the waitlist?
      We will continue placing students from the waitlist until we have no more rooms to offer. Traditionally, this occurs the first or second week of September.
    • Do I get to pick my bed option if I am on the waitlist?
      No, if you are offered a bed from the waitlist, it will be the first bed that becomes available for your gender and classification.
    • I am on the Marietta waitlist but  I want to change to the Kennesaw waitlist. How do I do that?
      First-Year students are not able to change their campus waitlist as it is determined by your declared major. Upper Class students would need to email ksuhousing@kennesaw.edu to change their campus waitlist selection.
