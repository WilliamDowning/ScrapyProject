Emily Taylor Bowman
Woodstock, GA

I had a good time the 4 years I've been at the Academy. Every class I have taken had a nice professor. I got along with all my professors and both advisors. I've also gotten along with everyone that has an office. The Commons was a great place to eat!

Nigel Louis Carter
Avondale Estates, GA

My time here at KSU was great! I have been able to discover so much.  I was great to experience life away from home and live as a college student.  KSU felt like another day in paradise.  Whenever classes got a little tough, I would motivate myself to push through.

Tyler Scott Crawford
Cartersville, GA

The Academy has meant so much to me over these past 4 years. I have learned so much and made friends along the way, and I don’t think I would be where I am today.  When I first started 4 years ago, I was very excited to see what college life was going to be like. I am so thankful for all of the help I have received to get into a career I have wanted to work for. I will never forget all the fun memories we have made with the Academy especially the fun events.

Carter James Hall
Johns Creek, GA

My Experience at the Academy has been great! I have made a lot of great friends, learned a lot, and have advocated for myself more. I also tried things that I have never done before, like standing up for myself.

 

Matthew David Holz
Alpharetta, GA

I enjoyed the academy a lot because I got to live on campus and experience college life which I absolutely love. I enjoyed meeting all kinds of people in the Academy and outside the Academy while being at KSU. I am going to visit KSU over and over when I graduate and spend the day on campus.

Jackson Scott Hurst
Kennesaw, GA

My Academy experience has been good and helpful. I have grown so much since I started here in 2018. I also now have a better idea of what I want to do in life.
