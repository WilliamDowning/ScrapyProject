                  • Spotlight Tour: MEBUS Program

                    Join us in learning more about the Joel A. Katz Music & Entertainment Business (MEBUS) Program at Kennesaw State. The Joel A. Katz Music & Entertainment Business (MEBUS) Program is an 18-credit hour minor or 24-credit hour certificate program offering three capstone courses in the music & entertainment business as well as marketing, management, production, communication, and entrepreneurship courses that prepare students for careers in the entertainment industry. MEBUS is open to all KSU undergraduate students with a desire to work in the entertainment industry in areas such as film and television production, audio and video technology, venue and artist management, and concert and event production.

                      Short on time but want to learn more about transferring to KSU?  We know your time is valuable!  Join the Transfer Team as we take you on a mini tour of the Kennesaw Campus.                       • Spotlight Tour: K-12 Teacher Education

                        Join us in learning more about the K-12 Teacher Education Program at Kennesaw State. After your campus tour, you are invited to a special tour of the Bagwell College of Education. The Bagwell College is one of the largest and strongest education programs in Georgia offering bachelors, masters, specialists, and doctor of education degree programs. Our programs are accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges (SACSCOC) and the Georgia Professional Standards Commission (GaPSC). We offer an extensive array of distinctive programs to prepare you to enter the classroom ready to impact the future! You will love our faculty and staff. They are caring, compassionate and committed to your success!
